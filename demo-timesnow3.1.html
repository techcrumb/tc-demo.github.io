Alia Bhatt buys flat in Mumbai for over Rs 13 crore, pays double the price of the actual cost - details here

Entertainment
Updated Jan 29, 2019 | 17:37 IST | Zoom TV Digital

Alia Bhatt, who is busy with the promotions of Gully Boy, recently bought a property in Mumbai, but paid double the cost of the original price.

Alia Bhatt buys a new property in Juhu  |  Photo Credit: Instagram

Alia Bhatt had a successful 2018 as her lone release, Raazi, not just did surprisingly well at the box office but also helped her garner critical appreciation. And now, the actress has started 2019 on an exciting note as well. While she is gearing up for the release of her next, Gully Boy, the actress has also bought a new flat in Mumbai’s Juhu area.

According to a report in DNA, the property is situated on the first floor of a swanky building in Juhu, and Alia paid a whopping Rs 13.11 crore to purchase it. Interestingly, according to the report in the daily, the original cost of the flat stands at Rs 7.86 crore.

Sangeet Hemant Kumar, of SHK Ventures, was quoted as saying in the report, “There's a premium attached to these properties and going by the rate, the price that Bhatt paid for this property isn't very high. The property is exactly like a house a celebrity would like to invest in.” Also read: Alia Bhatt's poise to Sunny Leone's hotness, here are all the shots from Dabboo Ratnani's 2019 calendar

Alia is also the owner of two more Juhu properties that she bought in 2015 from Anupam Kher and Kirron Kher. While one was for Rs 5.16 crore, the other costed Rs 3.83 crore.

On the work front, Alia is currently busy with the promotions of Gully Boy, which is her first on0screen collaboration with Ranveer Singh. The film, which will release on February 14, has been directed by Zoya Akhtar. The trailer and some songs of the film have already been released and have been immensely liked by fans.



On the other hand, Alia recently wrapped up Kalank, and is also working on Brahmstra, in which she will be seen with her real-life lover Ranbir Kapoor.
