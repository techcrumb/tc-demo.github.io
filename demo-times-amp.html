EXCLUSIVE| Jonita Gandhi on latest single Taajub Hai: It allowed me to showcase my personality

Updated Nov 19, 2018 | 14:33 IST | Amman Khurana

Singer Jonita Gandhi, the voice behind popular songs such as Pinjra, The Breakup Song and Gilehriyaan, has come up with Taajub Hai, her new single.

Jonita Gandhi in a still from Taajub Hai
Jonita Gandhi in a still from Taajub Hai

Singer Jonita Gandhi, the voice behind popular songs such as Pinjra, The Breakup Song and Gilehriyaan, has come up with a new single. Titled Taajub Hai, the song is about a girl who is surprised by the strange ways of the heart. The theme of the song may sound a bit serious but Taajub Hai, essentially, is a fun and quirky track. The song and music video is produced by JetSynthesys' Music Boutique.

“Taajub Hai is about a girl who is singing to her heart. Our heart gives us the most confusion. The song is saying that the heart can really surprise you,” says Jonita in an exclusive conversation with Zoom TV Digital.

What made her choose the song?

“This song is composed by Gulraj Singh and written by Manoj Yadav. This team is like a dream team in my career. I know them for a very long time but I never got to work with them together, as a team. This song was very relatable for everybody including myself. It allowed me to showcase my personality,” she explains.

If she ever had to say no to a song, what could be the possible reasons?

Loading ...

“Because I like to experiment a lot, there are not many songs that I wouldn’t like to sing. Having said that, I definitely look at the lyrics and the style of the song. I always try to look for new ways to sing a song,” she tells us.

Despite having lived in Canada for a considerable part of her life, how did her Hindi sound so clear in songs?

“I grew up singing in Hindi, so I am used to all the sounds. Moreover, I make sure whoever is recording me, is very picky,” she reveals.
