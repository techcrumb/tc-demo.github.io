Modi's Govt. Worst for the Farmers in the History of Independent India: Ashok Dhawale
Interview with Ashok Dhawale "05 Jun 2018
Interviewed by Sonali Produced by Newsclick Team,
Newsclick talks to Ashok Dhawale, National President of All India Kisan Sabha on the current Agrarian crisis. He discusses the condition of farmers during the four years of Modi regime and why they have been protesting. He states that the farmers are determined to fight back the oppressive nature of this government.
